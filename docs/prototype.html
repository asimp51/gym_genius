Protein Pancakes

High Protein Quick Breakfast
Calories
520 kcal
Protein
42g
Prep Time
5 min
Cook Time
10 min
Servings
2
Carbs / Fat
48g / 14g

Ingredients

• 1 cup rolled oats
• 2 scoops whey protein
• 2 eggs
• 1 banana
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Blend oats into flour
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
  3. Heat pan on medium, add batter
  4. Cook 2-3 min per side until golden
  5. Top with berries and honey

Tips

Make extra batter and refrigerate for up to 2 days. These also freeze well - make a batch on Sunday for the whole week!
